Simon Okbagergis Manna


Mr. Simon Okbagergis Manna is Software Developer and Machine Learning Engineer. He has over 9 years experience in Enterprise software development and Machine learning. Mr. Simon is founder and Editor in Chief of MachineLearningBusiness.com, a community of applied machine learning into business.


Mr. Simon has served as a machine learning engineer and consultant for many projects in different companies. He has been working on Tigrigna-English translation research projects with collaboration of Dr. Yemane Keleta (Phd in Natural Language processing).


Mr. Simon Manna has worked as lead technical coordinator of Edtech(Educational Technologies) Lab which creates innovative educational technologies to different colleges and institutions. Later Mr. Simon served as a managing director of Students Discovery and Innovation center. That center teaches students Robotics an programming at a very high speed and efficiency.


Since 2013 Mr. Simon has been working as a senior software developer and systems architect for different companies and organizations. He has developed Point of Sale systems (POS), Electronic medical systems, pharmaciutical systems and other projects. Since 2021, Mr. Simon is working as a senior IT consultant and software developer for WHO.


Mr. Simon is multiple national competitions winner including CEO of the second best national startup project winner.
